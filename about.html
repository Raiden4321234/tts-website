About Me and TTS

Our Story

TTS started as a passion project driven by my natural tendency to be everyone’s go-to person for help within my family and friends circle. I’ve always enjoyed lending a hand, and this passion for assisting others is what sparked the journey behind TTS.

My Qualifications

I’m currently studying IT through Open Polytechnic, loving the course, and excelling in it. I’m excited to apply my growing skills to help the Taupo community while earning some extra income—all with my "No fix, no pay" promise.

The Cave NZ

Beyond TTS, I proudly manage The Cave NZ, an awesome place where I’ve sharpened my tech support skills. Mention you heard about The Cave here when we meet in person, and you’ll get a special discount on my services!

What We Offer

Whether it’s setting up a new computer, creating a Facebook account, downloading mobile apps, or tackling bigger tech challenges, I’m here to offer affordable, friendly support. No problem is too small or too big—if I can help, I’ll give it my all! Ready to get started? Contact Me Today!
